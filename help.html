How to Install?

1.Visit our Download Center

2.Pick your Device

3.Choose Stable or Beta

4.Download it and Transfer to your Device

5.Boot in your Custom Recovery

6.Choose the Zip and Install it!

7.Reboot and That's it

❌-That Developers/Maintainer discontinued his work for that device.

✔️-That Developers/Maintainer is working for that device.
